Welcome PSUnians!

This Course Evaluation System is designed to help you track your academic progress and receive personalized recommendations on your future course selections. By evaluating your grades, the system guides you on which subjects you may excel in and advises against those that might need further preparation. Take control of your academic journey and make informed decisions with the help of our system!
